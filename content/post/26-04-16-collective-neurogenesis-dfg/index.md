Lukas gave a public talk at the community science art project "Collective Neurogenesis" in Bonn, organized by Paula Urrutia, Diana Bungard, and the German Research Foundation (DFG). His talk focused on grid cells and was entitled "Wie das Gehirn Räume vermisst: Die geheimnisvollen Gitterzellen."
