We're happy that David Wolf receives BONFOR funding for a one-year research position in the lab!

<!--more-->

BONFOR is the central funding program of the Faculty of Medicine and offers scientists funding opportunities at almost all career stages. BONFOR aims to improve the framework conditions for young scientists and to support high-quality research projects.

BONFOR link: https://www.medfak.uni-bonn.de/en/research/research-funding/bonfor/bonfor-forschungsfoerderprogramm
