The Bonn Spatial Memory Lab is pleased to welcome Lorenz Jaek as a new member of the lab!

<!--more-->

Lorenz is currently completing an MSc in Neurosciences at the University of Bonn. In the Bonn Spatial Memory Lab, Lorenz investigates the relationship between aperiodic neural activity and ripple events in human intracranial recordings.
